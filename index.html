About Us

Umar Games is a platform founded to provide a safe, educational, and fun gaming environment for children and young adults.

Using the latest developments in web technology, we develop high-quality games that can be played via browser without any downloads. Our vision is to contribute to mental development with game mechanics that combine learning with entertainment.
